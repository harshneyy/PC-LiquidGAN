‚ÄĘ No inductive bias for physical processes.

Literature Review (5/5): LiquidGAN

‚ÄĘ Neural ODE-based generator for smooth latent evolution.

‚ÄĘ Fully data-driven; no PDE-based thermal constraints.
‚ÄĘ Thermal consistency evaluated only via image metrics (FID,

‚ÄĘ No zero-shot cross-domain validation.
Implication: Motivates Physics-Constrained and Zero-Shot extension
